Calculating Total Pressure

Total Pressure is calculated as an additional auxiliary variable following a solve. Calculate the total pressure when you require pressure data that includes the pressure associated with the velocity head.

Procedure

  1. Run a 3D Flow and Heat Transfer solution containing base variables of speed, temperature, and density.
  2. In the Auxiliary Variables section of the Model Setup tab, click the Calculate button next to Total Pressure.

Results

Total Pressure values are available as TotalPressure scalar values in Analyze mode.
