Sloping Blocks and Solar Radiation

Thin Sloping Blocks are totally opaque to radiation, they absorb no solar radiation.

Thick Sloping Blocks are made of prisms and cuboids, therefore, it is likely that solar absorption will not be correctly accounted for. See “Primitives and Solar Radiation” for information on how cuboids and prisms are handled differently.
