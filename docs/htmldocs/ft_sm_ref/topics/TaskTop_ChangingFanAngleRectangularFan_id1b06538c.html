Changing the Fan Angle of a Rectangular Fan

Angled fans can be created by rotating rectangular fans about a chosen axis.

Prerequisites

  • Flow Type must be Normal

  • Fan Power must not be activated.

Procedure

  1. Choose the direction to be rotated: None (default), X Axis or Y Axis. The fan local coordinates are referenced, therefore Z is always normal.
  2. Enter the angle of rotation between −90 and +90 degrees.


    As this is a rectangular fan, no swirl can be impressed on the flow.
