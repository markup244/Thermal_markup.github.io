2-Resistor Model

The 2-Resistor Model is the simplest representation generic to all package types, consisting of two cuboid layers.

The internal resistances to the board are included in the 2 resistances:

  • from the Junction to the Top (case) and

  • from the Junction to the Bottom (board on which it is mounted), see Figure 1.
