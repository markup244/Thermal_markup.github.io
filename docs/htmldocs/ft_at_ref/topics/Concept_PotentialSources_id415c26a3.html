Potential Sources

A Potential source activates a source of electric current. Joule Heating must be enabled.

You have a choice on how a Potential source is defined, as described below. When a current is defined, it can be positive or negative.

Potential Source Defined by Source/Volume

An electric current per unit volume.

Potential Source Defined by Source/Area

An electric current per unit area.

Potential Source Defined as a Total Source

The total amount of electrical current.

The total value is applied over the Source SmartPart to which the Source attribute is attached. The Source SmartPart may be a volume or a plane (collapsed).

Potential Source Defined as a Fixed Source

An electric potential (voltage).
