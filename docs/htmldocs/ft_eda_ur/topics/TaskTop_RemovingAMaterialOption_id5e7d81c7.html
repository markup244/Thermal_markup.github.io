Removing a Material Option

If the materials selection list needs tidying, you can remove options no longer required.

Procedure

  1. Click the [...] button next to the dropdown list of materials to open the Material Library Selector dialog box.
  2. Select the material to be removed in the Selected Items pane, then click the ‘−’ button.

    The item is then deleted from the Selected Items list.

  3. Click Save to Config to remove the item from the materials selection list and click OK to close the dialog box.
