TEC Construction

A TEC is represented in the GUI as two abutting cuboids: one each for the hot and cold sides.

The two cuboids are required to ensure there are two rows of cells to hold the hot side and cold side temperatures.

In reality, a TEC can be thought of a being composed of three layers: two outer ceramic layers and an inner layer of Peltier elements. For the purposes of the model image this is simplified as two layers, however, the thickness of the outer ceramic layers is defined in the property sheet. This is identical for both layers but is not shown in the model image. The ceramic thickness must be less than half of the total thickness of the TEC.
