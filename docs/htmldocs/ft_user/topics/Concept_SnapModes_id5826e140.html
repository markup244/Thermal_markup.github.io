Snap Modes

Snap objects to the snap grid, or snap objects to other objects.

Tip

You can use Alt+arrow keys to move objects incrementally.

Snap to Grid

As you move objects, they are drawn towards the snap grid.

As you resize objects, the point you are dragging is drawn towards the snap grid.

Snapping towards the grid location works whether the grid is displayed or not.

Snap to Object

As you move objects, they will snap to the edge of other objects when they are moved within a certain tolerance. If an object is not within the tolerance, the moved object will be placed as though the snap is off.
