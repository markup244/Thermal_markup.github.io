Compact Models

How to obtain a single heat transfer coefficient value, that can be applied to the compact model base cuboid.

The results from a detailed analysis of a heat sink can be used in the surface exchange attribute settings to create a compact model.

The convective and conductive heat transfer coefficients are listed in the Cuboid Fluxes table (a Solid Conductors results table shown in Analyze mode). The heat transfer coefficients are for the base cuboid of the detailed heat sink SmartPart at the interface between the base and the fins, see Figure 1.
