Un-Highlighting Non-Planar Faces

By default, non-planar faces are highlighted in magenta.

Procedure

  1. To un-highlight non-planar faces, click the Preferences icon or choose Edit > Preferences to open the Preferences dialog box.
  2. Uncheck the Highlight Non Planar Faces check box and click OK.
