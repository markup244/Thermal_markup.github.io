Potting Compound

Potting compounds represent the material applied to seal components on a board. They can be placed over all or part of either side of the board. Multiple non-overlapping potting compounds are allowed.

The potting compound is always located as the last entry in the parent board node to prevent any overwriting by the further addition of components.

By default, a typical epoxy overmold, with a thermal conductivity of 0.68 W/mK, is applied to cover the top surface of the selected MotherBoard or DaughterBoard up to a height of 10 mm.

The material can be replaced with one defined by the user or loaded from the supplied Resins material library.

To create a potting compound, select the board to be sealed and click the Create Potting Compound icon. The potting compound appears as a red mesh in the display area.
