Enclosure Walls With Holes

Holes can be added to enclosure walls to allow the specification of airflow in and out.

Add holes in the enclosure walls for locating vents and fans.

Holes that are 0.01% or less than the smallest side of the enclosure wall are ignored by the grid, the solver, and the decompose command, and are only displayed in wireframe rendering mode.
