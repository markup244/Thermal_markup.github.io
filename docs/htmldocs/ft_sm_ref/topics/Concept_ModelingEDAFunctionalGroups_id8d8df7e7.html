Modeling EDA Functional Groups

The EDA Functional Group SmartPart is equivalent to the Functional Group modeling object in EDA Bridge.

An EDA Functional Group (Figure 1 is an example) contains EDA Components that belong to the functional group.

Figure 1. EDA Functional Group (Lightweight On)

Extended EDA Functional Group

When Lightweight is switched off, an EDA Functional Group SmartPart extends to an assembly with an attached Material attribute, inherited from the Dielectric Material of the parent EDA Board.
