Monitor Points

Use Monitor Point SmartParts when you want to monitor variables (usually temperature) at specific positions in the solution domain.

Although they are not physical objects, that is, they have no dimensions, monitor points are treated as part of the geometry structure and are displayed in the drawing board as cross-hairs, of a fixed size to aid visibility.
