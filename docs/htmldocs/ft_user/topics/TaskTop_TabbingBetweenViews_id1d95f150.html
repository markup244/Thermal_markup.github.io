Tabbing Between Views

The TAB key provides a quick way of changing the active view.

Procedure

  1. Click a pane in the GDA.
  2. Press the TAB key repeatedly.

Results

The behavior depends on the view:

  • In Examiner view (single pane), the view cycles around Positive X, Positive Y, Positive Z, and Isometric with each key press.

  • In Grid view, each of the four panes becomes the active pane in turn.

  • In Split Horizontally view or Split Vertically view, each of the two panes becomes the active pane in turn.
