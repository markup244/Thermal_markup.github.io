Application of Transient Functions to Coefficients of Linear Sources

When transient functions are applied to sources, they can affect coefficients of linear sources.

By default, transient functions multiply the value, Vϕ, of a defined linear source:

Sϕ = Cϕ(Vϕ − ϕ)

The Apply Transient to Coefficient setting in the property sheet also applies the transient function to the coefficient, Cϕ, thereby applying the transient functions to the total source Sϕ. This can be useful for some transient applications, for example, Crenel Functions.
