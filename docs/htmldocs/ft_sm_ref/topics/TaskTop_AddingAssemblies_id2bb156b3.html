Adding Assemblies

Use assemblies to organize the objects in your model.

Procedure

To add a new (empty) assembly, select the parent assembly to hold the new assembly and click the Assembly icon in the New Object Palette.

Results

The new assembly appears in the tree as a child of the selected parent assembly (Figure 1).

Figure 1. A Parent-Child Assembly Hierarchy

Initially, a new assembly (containing no objects) exists only as a reference origin and does not appear in the drawing board. When objects are added to the assembly, then its bounding box, that is, the bounding box of all the objects contained within the assembly, and its local coordinate system are displayed in the drawing board when the assembly is selected.

Moving the assembly, moves all of the objects that are children of the assembly.
