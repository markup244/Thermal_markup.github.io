Generic CRAC Units

Three generic assemblies are provided: Supply Not Turned, Supply Turned Horizontal, and Unit External.
Figure 1. Generic CRAC Units

The Supply Not Turned and Supply Turned Horizontal assemblies use a cuboid to represent the body of the unit and a Cooler SmartPart to represent the fans and coils. The Supply Not Turned assembly has vertical exhaust and supply. The Supply Turned Horizontal assembly has a vertical exhaust and a horizontal supply (that is, it assumes that turning vanes are used to turn the flow).

The Unit External assembly does not have a physical body but can be used to represent a CRAC unit that is outside the solution domain, that is, its supply and exhaust should be placed on the edge of the domain.

All the generic units have a default flow rate of 8.5 m³/s and maximum cooling load of 80 kW is set. The set point for the supply air is 15°C. Monitor points are provided on the supply and return.

The return air is at the top of the unit and supply is assumed to be beneath the floor (y = 0).
