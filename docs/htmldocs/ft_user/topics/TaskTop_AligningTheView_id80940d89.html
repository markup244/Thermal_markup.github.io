Aligning the View

The view can be aligned along one of the major axis or with the gravity vector.

Procedure

You have a choice:

If you want to...

Do the following:

Align the view along a major axis.

Choose one of the Viewer > View menu options or press one of the view shortcut keys.

The view is set to the chosen direction and is refitted. For example, choosing Viewer > Positive Z View or pressing the Z key displays the scene viewed from the positive z-axis direction.

Align the view so that gravity is vertically downwards.

Choose Viewer > Align With Gravity.
