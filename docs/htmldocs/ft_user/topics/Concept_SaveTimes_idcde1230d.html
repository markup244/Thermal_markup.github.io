Save Times

3D field data of transient analyses can be recorded at times other than the end of solution and will then be available for post-processing in Analyze mode.

The data is saved at “Save Times”, which can be specified in terms of time steps, at a specific time, or a combination of both.

Figure 1. Save Times at Every Fourth Time Step

After a solution has completed, the results are available in Analyze mode, but derived properties, such as flow rates and heat fluxes that are available for steady-state analyses, are not available for transient analyses.
