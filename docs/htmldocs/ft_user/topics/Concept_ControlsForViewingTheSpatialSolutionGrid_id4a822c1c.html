Controls for Viewing the Spatial Solution Grid

The spatial solution grid is viewed on the Workplane.

The grid is shown in black when the background is light, and is shown in white when the background is dark.

The following are the controls available when viewing the grid:

  • The Direction normal to the Workplane.

  • The Position of the Workplane along this direction.

    The position can also be moved, one cell at a time, by pressing the arrow keys.

  • Whether or not the grid is Projected onto the Workplane.

These controls are selectable from the toolbar.

When viewing the grid in Solid view you will not be able to see local grids. To see local grids you can either change to Wireframe view, see Figure 2, or use a Projected view (for example, see Figure 3.
