Context Rules for Overlapping Supplies and Extracts of Recirculation Devices

The context rules are dependent on whether the overlaps are from the same or from different recirculation devices.

  • If two or more supplies (or extracts) from the same recirculation device overlap, they override, but in such a away that the total mass flow required by that recirculation device is maintained.

  • If two or more supplies (or extracts) from different recirculation devices overlap, their flow rates are additive.
