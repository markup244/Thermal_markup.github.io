Modeling EDA Cans

The EDA Can SmartPart is equivalent to the Can modeling object in EDA Bridge.

An EDA Can is defined by its dimensions, material, and orientation on the board, see “EDA Can Property Sheet Construction Tab”.

Extended EDA Can

When Lightweight is switched off (Figure 1), an EDA Can SmartPart extends to:

  • An Enclosure SmartPart, see “Enclosures”. There is no Low Z wall as this is the side of the can that is mounted on the board.

  • A Volume Region SmartPart with attached Grid Constraint attributes.
