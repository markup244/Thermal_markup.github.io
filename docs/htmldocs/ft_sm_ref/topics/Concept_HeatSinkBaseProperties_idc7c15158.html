Heat Sink Base Properties

The size of the heat sink base is with reference to the heat sink origin.
Figure 1. Heat Sink Base Dimensions

The origin of the heat sink is located at the low coordinate corner of the base of the heat sink (Figure 1). The convention used is that the base of the heat sink is in the X-Y plane with the fins extruded in the Z-direction.

The base is directly represented as a block of conducting material. In a bonded fin heat sink, the base may be of a different material to the fins. The attributes of the base are that of the constituent material.
