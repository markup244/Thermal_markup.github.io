Types of Solution

Overviews of Flow and Heat Transfer, Flow Only and Conduction Only.

There are three types of solution:

  • Flow and Heat Transfer solves temperature, pressure, and velocities appropriate for the dimensionality selected (the default).

  • Flow Only calculates the flow of isothermal (constant temperature) fluid. Any thermal effects are ignored, as are gravity effects.

  • Conduction Only simulates the conduction of heat for the entire solution domain filled with stationary (non-flowing) material.

See Background of Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) in the Simcenter Flotherm Background Theory Reference Guide for details of the variables and their precise storage locations.
