Heat Sink Fabrication

There are two methods of fabrication: Extruded/Cast and Bonded

  • Extruded/Cast — constructs the fins from a single homogenous material and manufactured using an extrusion or casting process.

  • Bonded — constructs by bonding fins into grooves cut into the base of the heat sink. To enable the program to calculate the resistance effects, you specify the construction by setting the groove width and depth (see Figure 1), fin material and adhesive type.
