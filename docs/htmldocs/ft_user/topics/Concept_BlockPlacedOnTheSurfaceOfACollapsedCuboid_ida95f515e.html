Block Placed on the Surface of a Collapsed Cuboid

The heat transfer depends on the thermal model defined for the thermal attribute attached to the block.
Figure 1. Block Resting on a Collapsed Cuboid

  • Conduction thermal model.

    The collapsed cuboid reduces the heat transfer from the block to the fluid on the other side of the collapsed cuboid. If the thermal conductivity of the collapsed cuboid is set to zero, heat transfer is completely eliminated across the surface of intersection.

  • Fixed Heat Flow or Fixed Temperature thermal model.

    The heat flux from the block is unaffected by the collapsed cuboid.
